The Unseen Epidemic in Dance

This creates a dangerous paradox where the gift of flexibility becomes a liability, leading to chronic injury and shortened careers.

A Strategy for a Healthier Future

Stretch Beyond Limits stands at the intersection of science, medicine, and the arts. Our mission is to promote inclusion, accessibility, and awareness for hypermobile dancers through evidence-based programs built on three core pillars.

Advancing Medical Understanding

We bridge the gap between the laboratory and the dance studio by funding novel research into the underlying biology of hypermobility in athletes, safe training methods, and effective injury prevention protocols.

  • Fund grants for biomedical and clinical studies.
  • Develop a centralized database to track injury rates.
  • Establish evidence-based best practices for the dance industry.

Empowering Through Knowledge

Knowledge is the key to prevention. We empower dancers, teachers, and parents with the information they need to promote career longevity and reduce injury rates through accessible, accredited educational programs.

  • Create "Safe Stretching & Hypermobility" curriculum.
  • Host workshops at competitions and conventions.
  • Develop online resources on nutrition and conditioning.

Providing Tangible Support

No dancer should feel isolated. We provide direct services to improve access to quality care, provide financial assistance, and foster a strong sense of community for dancers who often feel misunderstood.

  • Establish scholarship funds for medical and training costs.
  • Build a national network of vetted medical professionals.
  • Offer advocacy services for accommodations.
